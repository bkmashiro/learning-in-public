## Philosophy

Most learning happens in private — notes rot in folders, insights evaporate, half-finished projects collect dust. **Learning in public** flips this: every paper read becomes a published analysis, every system understood becomes a minimal implementation, every algorithm learned becomes a visualization.

This creates a virtuous cycle:

- **Accountability** — a public streak is harder to break.
- **Sharing** — someone else benefits from your struggle.
- **Compound interest** — each piece links to others, and the portfolio grows more valuable over time.

This repository is the hub that ties all of it together.
